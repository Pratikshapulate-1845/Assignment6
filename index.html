About the City

Ahmednagar, officially Ahilyanagar, is a city in Maharashtra, India, serving as the headquarters of the Ahmednagar district.

Ahmednagar city famous for

Ahmednagar Fort is widely regarded as one of the most well-planned and impregnable forts in Maharashtra.

Population

The city of Ahmednagar, in the state of Maharashtra, India, had a population of approximately 350,859 as per the 2011 Indian Census.
