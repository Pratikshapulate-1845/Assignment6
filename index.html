Welcome to Ahmednagar City

About City

Ahmednagar, officially Ahilyanagar, is a city in Maharashtra, India, serving as the headquarters of the Ahmednagar district.

It is located about 120 km northeast of Pune and 120 km from Aurangabad.

Culture

The city is deeply rooted in the history of the Maratha Empire, the Mughal period, and even the British era, with notable landmarks like the Ahmednagar Fort.
